I am a Ph.D. student in the Computer Science and Engineering department at Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Bombay under Prof. Uday P. Khedker.
I joined IIT Bombay as a PhD student in July, 2014.

My research interest is in the area of static analysis of programs. So far, I have worked on various data flow analyses. In particular, I am working on improving the precision of data flow analyses by eliminating the effect of infeasible control flow paths without compromising on efficiency.

I believe, I am close to finishing Ph.D. :-).
