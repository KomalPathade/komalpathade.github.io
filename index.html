Work summary:

I work as a researcher since 2011 at TRDDC, one of the research-and-innovation labs of TCS Research (Tata Consultancy Services Ltd), located in Pune, India.

My research interests span across several aspects of static program analysis, Data Flow Analysis, software verification, and software engineering.
Currently, my primary focus is on problems at the intersection of machine learning and program analysis, verification. In particular, I am trying to
apply program analysis to prove the robustness of machine learning algorithms against data poisoning attacks: an attack where someone can add
malicious data to training set to get favourable outcome from the ML model.
